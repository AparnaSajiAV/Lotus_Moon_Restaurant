Welcome to Lotus Moon Restaurant

Experience the authentic flavors of Kerala in a warm and welcoming ambiance at Lotus Moon Restaurant. Indulge in our carefully crafted dishes that celebrate the richness of traditional spices, fresh ingredients, and centuries-old recipes. Whether it’s the tantalizing aroma of Malabar Biryani, the comforting taste of Kerala Sadhya, or the irresistible sweetness of Palada Payasam, every bite is a journey through the heart of Kerala.

Perfect for family dinners, casual meetups, or special occasions, Lotus Moon promises an unforgettable culinary experience. Let the flavors of Kerala light up your taste buds. Come, savor the magic under the Lotus Moon!

Come as guests, leave as family.

About Us

At Lotus Moon Restaurant, we believe in bringing people together over good food and great memories. Nestled in the heart of [Location], we are your gateway to the vibrant and authentic flavors of Kerala. Our journey began with a passion for preserving the culinary traditions of Kerala and sharing them with the world. From the aromatic spices of Malabar to the coastal delicacies of Alleppey, our menu is a tribute to the rich heritage and diversity of Kerala’s cuisine. Each dish is prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients and a generous dose of love, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience. Whether you’re savoring our signature Karimeen Pollichathu, indulging in a lavish Sadhya, or enjoying the warmth of a Sulaimani tea, we promise to transport you to the land of coconut palms and backwaters. At Lotus Moon, it’s not just about the food—it’s about the experience. Step into our cozy, welcoming ambiance and let us take you on a flavorful journey through Kerala.
